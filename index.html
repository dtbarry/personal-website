About Me

Hello, I’m David Barry.

I am passionate about using creativity, teamwork, and problem‑solving to serve others and contribute to meaningful, mission‑driven work. While my degree is in Computer Science and I’ve worked in web and software development, my journey has taken me far beyond technology. Along the way, I’ve led teams, trained employees, served in customer‑facing roles, and built programs that bring people together.

In high school, I started a photography club and a discussion group that grew into weekly gatherings for 10–30 people — spaces where we learned, created, and encouraged one another in living out faith and loving others. Later, I served as a staff supervisor and mountain guide at Spring Canyon, leading cadets from U.S. military academies through challenging treks and mentoring them along the way.

Professionally, I’ve worked everywhere from national laboratories to small creative agencies, and I’ve gained experience in customer service, employee training, and operations at organizations like Chick‑fil‑A, Best Buy, and the United States Postal Service. I’ve also had the opportunity to serve with Samaritan’s Purse in Boone, which strengthened my desire to be part of a team that makes a global impact for Christ.

What ties all of these experiences together is my adaptability, my love for people, and my drive to keep learning. Whether leading a team, creating solutions, or helping an organization run more smoothly, I thrive when I can bring energy, ideas, and encouragement to those around me.

I’m now seeking opportunities where I can combine my creative thinking, people skills, and technical background to help a mission‑driven organization grow and serve others well.
